## Ranking Rules

- Prioritize the latest 24-48 hours.
- Backfill with valuable items from the last 7 days when needed.
- Prioritize trade shows, policy, M&A, pricing, supply and demand, channel shifts, and product or agency opportunities.
- Soft copy, generic reports, and weakly related items can only be used as low-priority fillers.
