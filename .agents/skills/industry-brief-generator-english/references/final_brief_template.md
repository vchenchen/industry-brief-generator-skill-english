# Final Brief Template

Generate this only after the user clearly selects `final_count` item numbers.

## Text Version

```text
# {brand}

Date: {date}
Use case: {final_use}
Selected items: {selected_ids}

## Brand Area
{brand}

## Cover Headline
{cover_title}

## Quick Reads
- {quick_read_1}
- {quick_read_2}
- {quick_read_3}
- {quick_read_4}
- {quick_read_5}

## {final_count} News Cards

### 1. {title}
{summary}
Why it matters: {why_it_matters}
Internal observation or expansion angle: {angle}

## Main Theme
{mainline}

## Recommended Follow-ups
1. {recommendation}

## Social Title Options
- {title_option}

## Newsletter Title Options
- {title_option}

## Closing Line
{closing}

## Source Links
1. {source_title}: {url}
```

## Use-Case Adaptation

- `internal brief`: keep “why it matters”, “internal observation”, and “recommended follow-ups”; use a restrained tone.
- `customer update`: emphasize market impact, opportunities, and risks; avoid internal-only judgment.
- `newsletter`: use a stronger headline and clearer sectioning.
- `social post`: make it lighter and more scannable, but avoid hype.

## Poster Version

Poster cards should include only:

- brand
- date
- cover headline
- quick reads
- short news cards
- main theme
- footer brand

Poster cards should not include:

- source links
- source dates
- why-it-matters fields
- expansion angles
- long explanations
